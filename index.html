Yixuan Gao - 高翌轩

Researcher in Computer Science

I'm Yixuan Gao, a PhD candidate at Cornell Tech. My supervisor is Prof. Rajalakshmi Nandakumar, and my PhD committee includes Prof. Tanzeem Choudhury and Prof. Deborah Estrin.

Before coming to Cornell, I conducted my undergraduate research under Prof. Tim Brecht and Prof. Ali Abedi at the University of Waterloo.

My research interests are twofold:

On one hand, I am passionate about wireless sensing and communication, utilizing wireless and wearable sensing techniques to tackle health challenges. I also design solutions for environmental issues by integrating wireless technologies with AI/ML approaches. Additionally, I work on traditional wireless communication protocols, including WiFi.

On the other hand, I explore the privacy aspects of wireless technologies, with a focus on ethically conducting wireless sensing and communication. This includes ongoing collaborative work with Prof. Helen Nissenbaum.
